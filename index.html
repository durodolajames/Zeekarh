Questions answered

Everything you need to know.

Come with a clean face, free of makeup if possible. Bring a list of current skincare products you use, note any allergies or skin sensitivities, and have photos ready showing your skin concerns. We'll handle the rest during your comprehensive analysis.

Most of our treatments have minimal to no downtime. Facials and light peels may cause slight redness for a few hours. Microneedling typically results in 24-48 hours of mild redness. We'll always provide specific aftercare instructions and recovery timelines before your treatment.

Results vary by treatment. Facials provide immediate glow, while chemical peels show full results within 5-7 days as skin renews. Microneedling results develop over 4-6 weeks as collagen builds. Your personalized routine will include a realistic timeline for your specific skin goals.

We recommend facials every 4-6 weeks for maintenance. Chemical peels are typically scheduled every 4-8 weeks depending on strength. Microneedling sessions are spaced 4-6 weeks apart for a series of 3-6 treatments. Your consultation will establish the perfect rhythm for your skin.

Yes, we customize every treatment to your specific skin type and concerns. Whether you have sensitive, oily, dry, or combination skin, we adjust product selection and treatment intensity accordingly. Your safety and comfort are our priority — we never use a one-size-fits-all approach.
